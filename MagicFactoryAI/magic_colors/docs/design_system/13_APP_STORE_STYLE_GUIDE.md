## 11. Marketing Channel Palette

When marketing on Instagram, TikTok, YouTube:

| Channel | Visual rules |
|---|---|
| Instagram | Use Tier-2 light palette. Single hero mascot per post. Avoid "real kids" photos — always Pixel + illustrations. |
| TikTok | High-saturation, 9:16 vertical, dynamic 3-second crops. Mascot must be in first 1 s. |
| YouTube | 16:9, color-corrected to Tier-1 brand. No talking heads. Mascot voiceover-chime + soft chimes only. |
| App preview video | 30 s, mixed scenes. |
| Newsletter | Soft Tier-2 palette + bullet iconography. Mascot is signature header. |

## 12. Anti-Marketing (do NOT use)

- ❌ Real children's faces in marketing.
- ❌ Comparisons to competitors by name.
- ❌ "Free" with implications of paid content lock.
- ❌ "Limited time" messaging.
- ❌ Loud jump-cuts in preview videos (dangerous for proprioception-sensitive children).
- ❌ Religious messaging of any kind.

## 13. App Store Update Cadence

- v1.0 launch (Sprint 4).
- v1.1 seasonal update (Sprint 6 — Wave 2 worlds).
- v1.2 retrospective update (Sprint 9 — Wave 3 + master brush).
- v1.3 localization + accessibility polish (Sprint 11).

Each update has its own screenshot set (relevant to the new content).

## 14. Customer Support Tone

- In-app help: chimes + mascot reassurance. No captcha.
- Email: friendly, structured, ≤ 5-step reply template.
- App Store replies: ≤ 24 h on weekdays. Replying with empathy first.
- Parents' email distinct from kids' email.
