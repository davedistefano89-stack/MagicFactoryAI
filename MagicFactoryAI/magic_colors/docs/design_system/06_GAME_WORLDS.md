**Document:** Official World Bible v1.0

## 1. World Mapping Philosophy

Each Magic Colors world is a curated **40-page coloring book** with its own:

- Visual palette (drawn from `02_COLOR_SYSTEM.md`).
- Ambient music loop (~ 90 s).
- Ambient SFX (birds, wind, water, etc.).
- Mascot variation (Pixel wears a costume — same body, different color).
- Unlock ladder (free starter pages + paid expansion packs).
- Star rating (1–3 stars per page).

A world is **not open world**. Worlds are linear: page 1 → page 40. This removes exploration anxiety.

## 4. Cross-World Rules

- **Voice/Music:** Each world has 1 music loop, 1 entry jingle (4 bars), 1 completion jingle (8 bars).
- **Anatomy:** Pixel's body never changes shape — only costume. This keeps the mascot instantly recognizable in every costumed world.
- **Difficulty curve:** Pages within a world progress from large simple shapes (Wave 1 first world) to small detail-heavy shapes (Wave 3 final world).
- **Word count:** Children-facing copy ≤ 8 words per page.
- **No real-world currency references** in any world.

## 5. Star Rating System

Per-page up to 3 stars based on:

| Criteria | Star 1 | Star 2 | Star 3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Coloring coverage | ≥ 30 % | ≥ 70 % | ≥ 95 % |
| Color variety | ≥ 3 colors | ≥ 6 colors | ≥ 10 colors |
| Time on page | ≥ 30 s | ≥ 60 s | ≥ 120 s |

(Total stars are kept private to a single session; aggregate progress shown as % completion.)

## 6. World Unlocked Pop-Up

When a world is unlocked, show a pop-up:

1. World illustration fills 80 % of screen.
2. World title `displayXl`.
3. Mascot costume silhouettes border.
4. CTA "Lets color!" big button.

Sound: world entry jingle + chime.

## 7. World Locked Card

When a world is locked:

1. World illustration 80 % opacity grayscale.
2. Soft padlock silhouette in top-right corner.
3. Requirement text "Color X pages of Unicorn Valley first."

No countdowns, no "limited time" timers, no fear.

## 8. Asset Manifestation per World

Each world ships with: 40 page SVGs, 1 background, 1 music loop, 5 ambient SFX, 1 entry jingle, 1 completion jingle, 1 mascot costume variant.

Total payload per world (compressed): ~ 12 MB. World 1 ships as `world_unicorn_valley_v1.zip`.

## 9. Wave Release Windows
